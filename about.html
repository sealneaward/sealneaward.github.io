About Me

Résumé

 I got into data science and prediction models after I took data management systems for the first time. Our final project was a simple web application that would utilize a MySQL server and insert real time traffic from a REST API using PHP. Although the initial task was very interesting, our group decided to go beyond the project requirements and looked into locating areas of Toronto that had a high probability of traffic occurring there. The algorithm we created was very simple, but that project really sparked my interest in the endless possibilities that came with data analysis. After that I started looking up techniques and technologies used in data science and enlisted in the Data Science specialization offered through Coursera to learn more about the field. The work I have done so far in the specialization was so much fun to do.

My name is Neil Seward and I'm currently entering my fourth and final year at the University of Ontario Institute of Technology (UOIT). Over the years at UOIT and through outside development efforts, I have grown into a software developer with a range of skills and aptitudes. Entering my last year, I really hope to learn as much as possible and really create something beautiful with my peers in our capstone project. Our team is currently seeking professors to team with for our project and exploring different topics. Ideally, I would like to work on a topic that involves data analysis.
