Filming this Year

Posted by Neil Seward on December 2nd 2019

This was my last year filming. It was definitely the most intense year I spend filming. I was fortunate enought to travel to Virginia, Washington DC, North Carolina, Detroit, and all over Ontario for this year's project.

Filming with everyone was great. A lot of really dedicated skaters in Oshawa. The scene was really nice to accept me as a filmer for the area for the last few years and I will always be great for that. I've bought a house recently and won't have the time or the resources to film like I used to.

Please enjoy this last project of mine. Everyone worked really hard on it. I hope you enjoy it.
